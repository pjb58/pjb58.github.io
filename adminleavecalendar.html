Administration Staff Leave Calendar

The Administration Staff Leave Calendar is a software application designed to help managers and administrators track employee leave schedules in an easy-to-use format. It provides a clear and structured view of leave allocations, helping with planning and operational continuity.

Key Features

  • Displays an annual leave calendar for all administration staff.
  • Shows employee names and associated company details.
  • Clearly marks different types of leave (e.g., Annual Leave, Sick Leave) using color-coded indicators.
  • Automatically pulls leave data from the payroll system.
  • Organizes data into a structured grid format, making it easy to view leave schedules at a glance.
  • Allows filtering and sorting of employee records for better analysis.

How It Works

When the application is launched, it automatically retrieves and displays employee leave data for the entire year. Each day in the calendar is labeled with the corresponding leave type, helping managers quickly identify leave patterns.
